About this Web Site

 

I am a resident of Lantana and I live in the Garner subdivision.  The weather station providing this data is a WeatherFlow Tempest Weather Station located in Garner.

The Tempest Weather Station providing the data for this website uses haptic sensors, so the data collected is mostly accurate.  Temperature and Humidity, brightness, UV etc., that can be accurately measured with digital sensors are all accurate, however things like rain and wind that are typically measured with analog sensors may not be 100% accurate.
